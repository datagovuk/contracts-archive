WELCOME TO THE CONTRACTS ARCHIVE

WARNING: This is archived data.

This site is a read only archive of historic contract data from the Business Link Contracts Finder website that closed in February 2015.

This site includes public sector contract tenders and contracts awarded between 11/2/2011 and 25/2/2015.

The site was superseded by a new Contracts Finder designed and operated by the Crown Commercial Service.

This data will not be updated and does not contain contracts which have been awarded since 26/2/2015. There was no batched copying of data from the old site to the new site, but there may have been transfers on a case-by-case basis.

Currently this site does not list contracts above the OJEU threshold, but it will be included in the future. Information about these contracts can be found on the TED site.

This archive has been provided by the data.gov.uk team using data from the Crown Commercial Service.
